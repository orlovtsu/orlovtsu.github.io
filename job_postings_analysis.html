Methodology

1. Data Cleaning & Preparation



Web scraping using Selenium and BeautifulSoup in two stages:

- scraping job titles, company names and URLs to job posting;

- scraping job descriptions, skill sets, company size, location, workplace type, job level, how long it was posted, how many applicants

3,659 job postings were collected, almost 200 lost because they were lost next day after the 1st step

Job title and Job level labeling in the entire dataset

Cleaning to only 8 most popular types of jobs: Data Scientist, Data Engineer, Data Analyst, Machine Learning Engineer, Software Engineer, Product Manager, Business Intelligence and Data Entry

2,748 rows after cleaning and filtering
